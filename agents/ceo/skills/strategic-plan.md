# Skill: Strategic Planning

## When to Use
- At company startup (first run)
- Every 6 hours for strategic review
- When a major pivot is needed (revenue stalled, market shift)

## Initial Strategy Creation

### Phase 1: Discovery (First 30 minutes)
1. Scan current market landscape with web research
2. Identify 5-10 potential revenue streams
3. Score each using the Revenue Scan framework
4. Select top 2-3 to pursue simultaneously

### Phase 2: Resource Allocation
Assign agents based on top opportunities:
- CTO: Build MVP for highest-scored opportunity
- BizDev: Validate demand for 2nd opportunity, start outreach for 1st
- Ops: Set up monitoring, cost tracking from minute one

### Phase 3: Execute & Measure
- Set 4-hour checkpoints
- At each checkpoint: review progress, revenue, costs
- Be ruthless: kill anything that isn't working after 2 checkpoints

## Strategic Review (Every 6 Hours)

1. **Revenue Check**: Are we making money? How much? From what?
2. **Cost Check**: What are we spending? Is it efficient?
3. **Agent Performance**: Who's delivering? Who's stuck?
4. **Market Check**: Has anything changed? New opportunities?
5. **Pivot Decision**: Should we double down, diversify, or pivot?
