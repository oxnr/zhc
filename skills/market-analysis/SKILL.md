# Skill: Market Analysis

## Purpose
Evaluate market opportunities for revenue potential.

## Framework
For each opportunity, assess:

### 1. Demand Signal
- Are people searching for this? (Google Trends, Reddit, forums)
- Are people paying for alternatives? (competitor analysis)
- Are people complaining about current solutions?

### 2. Competition
- Who else is doing this?
- What are they charging?
- What are their weaknesses?
- Can we differentiate with speed, price, or AI-native approach?

### 3. Revenue Model
- Subscription (best): Monthly recurring revenue
- One-time purchase: Quick cash but no compounding
- Freemium: Build audience, convert later
- Marketplace/commission: Take % of transactions

### 4. Build Complexity
- Can CTO ship this in < 4 hours?
- Do we need external APIs or data?
- Any regulatory concerns?

## Output
```
## Market Analysis: [Opportunity Name]
- Demand: [HIGH/MED/LOW] — [evidence]
- Competition: [HIGH/MED/LOW] — [evidence]
- Revenue Model: [type] — estimated [$/mo]
- Build Time: [hours]
- Overall Score: [X/25]
- Recommendation: [PURSUE/EXPLORE/PASS]
```
