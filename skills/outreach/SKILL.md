# Skill: Outreach

## Purpose
Reach potential customers and close deals.

## Channels
1. **Email**: Direct outreach to prospects
2. **Social DMs**: Twitter/LinkedIn for warm leads
3. **Communities**: Reddit, Discord, Slack communities
4. **Product Hunt**: For product launches
5. **Hacker News**: For technical products (Show HN)

## Outreach Protocol
1. Research the target (company, person, pain point)
2. Personalize the message (reference their specific situation)
3. Lead with value (free insight, helpful tip)
4. Soft pitch (not salesy)
5. Clear CTA (book a call, try free, reply)
6. Follow up once after 3 days if no response
7. Never follow up more than twice

## Message Template Structure
```
[Personalized opener — shows you did your research]
[Value statement — what you can do for them]
[Social proof — if any (products shipped, results)]
[Soft CTA — low friction next step]
```

## Tracking
Log every outreach in memory:
```
- OUTREACH: [Target] | [Channel] | [Status: sent/replied/converted] | [Timestamp]
```
