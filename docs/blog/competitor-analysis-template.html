Free Competitor Analysis Template (+ How to Automate It)

A framework you can use right now to analyze any competitor. Copy it, fill it in, and start making better decisions today.

The template

Below is the framework we use at ZeroIntel for every competitive intelligence report. You can run through it manually for free, or let our AI do it in 24 hours. Either way, this is the structure that produces actionable intelligence.

Where to find it: Pricing page. If "contact us" pricing, check G2 reviews (users often mention pricing) or ask a sales rep.

What to look for: Who's cheapest at each tier? What features get gated behind higher tiers? Is there a free tier and how restricted is it? Any startup/discount programs?

Where to find it: Features page, documentation, changelog, product tours.

Pro tip: Mark each cell as Yes, No, Partial, or Paid tier only. The nuance matters. A feature that exists only on the enterprise plan is different from one that's available on free.

Section 4: Positioning analysis

For each competitor, answer these questions:

  1. What problem do they claim to solve? (Read their homepage hero section)
  2. Who do they target? (Check case studies, testimonials, industries listed)
  3. What's their unique differentiator? (What do they emphasize that others don't?)
  4. What's their content angle? (Blog topics, webinar subjects, social media themes)
  5. What objections do they preempt? (FAQ page is gold for this)

The insight you're looking for: Where is the positioning overlap? If three companies all say "simple, powerful, and easy to use," none of them are differentiated. The gap in the market is the message nobody's claiming.

Section 5: Customer sentiment

Source You Comp 1 Comp 2

Where to find it: G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, Reddit, Twitter. Read the 1-star and 2-star reviews first. They tell you where the product fails.

Section 6: Action items

This is the section that makes the entire exercise worthwhile. After filling in sections 1-5, answer:

  1. Price: Are we over- or under-priced relative to features delivered?
  2. Features: What do competitors offer that we're missing? Which gaps are urgent?
  3. Positioning: How do we differentiate from what everyone else claims?
  4. Weaknesses: Where are competitors weak that we can attack in sales collateral?
  5. Threats: What is the most dangerous competitor trend we need to respond to?

Rank each action by impact (high/medium/low) and effort (high/medium/low). Do the high-impact, low-effort items this week.

How long this takes manually

If you're thorough, filling in this template for 3 competitors takes 6-10 hours. You need to:

  • Read through each competitor's entire website
  • Compare pricing across different page formats
  • Read dozens of reviews on multiple platforms
  • Cross-reference features across documentation
  • Synthesize everything into recommendations

Most people start this process with good intentions and abandon it halfway through. The second competitor gets less attention than the first. The third barely gets a skim.
