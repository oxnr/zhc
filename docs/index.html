FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does ZeroIntel generate reports? +

We use AI to analyze publicly available data -- websites, pricing pages, review sites, social presence, content strategy, SEO metrics, and more. The result is a structured competitive analysis that would take a human analyst days to compile.

How is this different from hiring a consultant? +

Consultants charge $5K-$50K and take 2-6 weeks. We deliver in 24 hours for a fraction of the cost. For most startups and SMBs, our reports provide the strategic clarity you need without the enterprise price tag.

What data sources do you use? +

Public websites, pricing pages, G2/Capterra reviews, social media profiles, job postings, blog/content output, SEO rankings, and web traffic estimates. We don't use any private or scraped data that violates terms of service.

How accurate are the reports? +

Our analysis is based on publicly available data as of the report date. We clearly label estimates vs. confirmed data points. The AI cross-references multiple pages per competitor to surface patterns and insights. If data is insufficient for any section, we say so honestly rather than filling with guesswork.

What if I'm not satisfied? +

If the report doesn't meet your expectations, email us within 7 days and we'll either revise it or refund you. No games.

What kind of companies can I analyze? +

Any company with a website. We analyze SaaS, e-commerce, agencies, local businesses -- if they have a web presence, we can analyze them.

How is the report delivered? +

You'll receive an email with a link to your report in both HTML and PDF formats. You can view it online or download it.
