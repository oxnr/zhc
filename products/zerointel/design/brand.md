# ZeroIntel Brand Guide

## Logo
- Text-based: "Zero" (white) + "Intel" (indigo-400 #818cf8)
- Font: Inter, weight 700
- No icon — text mark only

## Colors
- **Primary**: Indigo 600 (#4f46e5) — CTAs, accents, brand actions
- **Indigo 400**: #818cf8 — logo highlight, lighter accents
- **Violet 500**: #8b5cf6 — gradients paired with indigo
- **Background**: Slate 950 (#020617) → Indigo 950 (#1e1b4b) radial gradient
- **Surface**: rgba(255,255,255,0.03) with rgba(255,255,255,0.06) borders
- **Text Primary**: #f8fafc
- **Text Secondary**: #94a3b8
- **Text Muted**: #64748b
- **Emerald 400**: #34d399 — success, checkmarks, positive scores
- **Amber 400**: #fbbf24 — warnings, medium scores, stars
- **Rose 400**: #fb7185 — alerts, low scores, high-priority

## Typography
- **Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: 700-800 weight, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em to -0.03em)
- **Body**: 400 weight, 0.875-0.9375rem, line-height 1.6
- **Labels**: 500-600 weight, 0.6875-0.75rem, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.06-0.1em

## Design Tokens
- Border radius: 0.75rem (cards), 1rem (larger panels), 999px (badges/pills)
- Card hover: translateY(-2px) + shadow increase
- Glow effect: box-shadow 0 0 50px rgba(99,102,241,0.25)
- Grid pattern: 64px grid, rgba(99,102,241,0.03) lines

## Direction
- Dark, professional, data-driven
- "Bloomberg meets Stripe" aesthetic
- Premium but approachable
- Minimal, clean, high contrast
- Trust through design consistency
