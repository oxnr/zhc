# ZeroIntel Launch Plan

## Pre-Launch Checklist (Before Day 0)

- [ ] Landing page live and accessible
- [ ] Gumroad products created (Starter $49, Pro $99, Enterprise $249)
- [ ] Gumroad checkout links embedded in landing page
- [ ] Report generation script tested with real URLs
- [ ] Sample report generated for showcase
- [ ] Outreach messages finalized (all 5 channels)
- [ ] Email templates personalized for first batch
- [ ] Discount codes created (REDDIT20, INDIEHACKER30, LAUNCH20)

## Launch Sequence

### Day 0: Soft Launch (Community Seeding)

**Morning (9-11am ET)**
1. Post on IndieHackers (highest conversion community for bootstrapped products)
   - Use IH post template from outreach-messages.md
   - Engage with every comment within 1 hour
   - Offer 30% discount code: INDIEHACKER30

2. Post on r/SaaS (most targeted subreddit)
   - Use Reddit post template
   - Must provide genuine value, not just pitch
   - Reply to every question
   - Offer 20% discount code: REDDIT20

**Afternoon (1-3pm ET)**
3. Twitter/X launch thread
   - Post the 5-tweet thread from outreach-messages.md
   - Tag relevant startup/marketing accounts
   - Pin the thread
   - Engage with every reply and quote tweet

4. LinkedIn post
   - Post professional version
   - Ask 5-10 connections to engage (comment/like) within first hour
   - Critical: first hour engagement determines LinkedIn reach

**Evening (5-7pm ET)**
5. Send first batch of cold emails (10 highest-priority prospects)
   - Use Template 1 (Direct Value Prop) for founders in competitive markets
   - Use Template 2 (Pain Point) for recently funded startups
   - Personalize each one — no mass send

### Day 1: Amplification

**Morning**
1. Show HN post (Hacker News)
   - Use Show HN template
   - Best posting time: 8-10am ET (weekday)
   - Stay online to answer questions for 3+ hours
   - Offer to generate free previews for anyone interested

2. Respond to all Day 0 engagement
   - Reply to every Reddit comment
   - Reply to every IH comment
   - Follow up on Twitter/X conversations
   - Connect with LinkedIn commenters

**Afternoon**
3. Send second batch of cold emails (10 more prospects)
   - Use Template 3 for marketing consultants/agencies
   - Personalize based on their client work

4. Cross-post any early traction
   - If anyone buys, share (with permission) on Twitter/X
   - "First customer just bought a report on [their market]. Here's what they said..."

### Day 2: Follow-Up Blitz

1. Follow-Up 1 on all Day 0 cold emails (3-day rule)
2. Post results/learnings on IndieHackers
   - "Day 2 of selling AI competitive reports. Here's what happened."
   - Transparency builds trust and drives more sales
3. Engage in competitor-analysis threads on Reddit/Twitter
   - Don't pitch — add value, mention tool naturally
4. Send third batch of cold emails (remaining prospects)

### Day 3-7: Sustained Outreach

1. Follow-Up 2 on Day 0 emails (7-day mark)
2. Follow-Up 1 on Day 1 emails
3. Daily engagement on all platforms (30 min/day minimum)
4. Identify new prospect leads from platform engagement
5. If we have any customers: ask for testimonials
6. Update landing page with any social proof

## Pre-Sell Strategy: 5 Customers in 48 Hours

### Tactic 1: Free Preview Hook
- Offer to run a free "executive summary" (1 page) for anyone interested
- Full report unlocked on purchase
- This lets prospects see the value before paying

### Tactic 2: Aggressive Launch Discounts
- INDIEHACKER30: 30% off (IH community)
- REDDIT20: 20% off (Reddit)
- LAUNCH20: 20% off (general)
- First 10 customers only — creates urgency

### Tactic 3: Reverse Risk
- Money-back guarantee (48h refund policy)
- "If the report isn't better than what you'd get from ChatGPT, full refund."
- Eliminates purchase anxiety at $49 price point

### Tactic 4: Direct Outreach with Personalization
- Research each prospect's competitive landscape before emailing
- Include 1-2 specific observations about their market in the email
- "I noticed [Competitor] just updated their pricing. Here's what we found..."

### Tactic 5: Build-in-Public Content
- Share the journey of building ZeroIntel on Twitter/X and IH
- People buy from makers they follow
- Share revenue numbers transparently
