## Prospect Qualification Criteria

A prospect is HIGH PRIORITY if they match 2+ of:
- Recently raised funding (last 6 months)
- Operate in a market with 5+ direct competitors
- Have posted publicly about competitive positioning challenges
- Are a marketing professional who serves startup clients
- Have a product that launched in the last 90 days

## Outreach Priority Order

1. **IndieHackers builders with revenue** — most likely to pay $49 immediately
2. **Recently funded startups** — have budget, need to show investors competitive awareness
3. **Product Hunt recent launchers** — top of mind, just entered competitive market
4. **Marketing consultants** — repeat buyers, will buy for multiple clients
5. **Founders posting about competition** — high intent, need a solution now
