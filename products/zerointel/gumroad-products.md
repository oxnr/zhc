# ZeroIntel Gumroad Product Setup

## Product 1: Starter Report ($49)
- **Name:** ZeroIntel Starter - Competitive Intelligence Report
- **URL slug:** zerointel-starter
- **Price:** $49
- **Description:** AI-powered competitive analysis of your company vs up to 3 competitors. Includes positioning analysis, pricing comparison, and feature gap analysis. Delivered within 24 hours.
- **Custom fields on checkout:**
  - Your Company URL (required, text)
  - Competitor URL 1 (required, text)
  - Competitor URL 2 (optional, text)
  - Competitor URL 3 (optional, text)
  - Email for delivery (required, email)

## Product 2: Pro Report ($99)
- **Name:** ZeroIntel Pro - Competitive Intelligence Report
- **URL slug:** zerointel-pro
- **Price:** $99
- **Description:** Comprehensive AI competitive analysis of your company vs up to 5 competitors. Includes everything in Starter plus SEO audit, strategic recommendations, and market sizing. Delivered within 24 hours.
- **Custom fields on checkout:**
  - Your Company URL (required, text)
  - Competitor URLs (up to 5, comma-separated) (required, text)
  - Email for delivery (required, email)

## Product 3: Enterprise Report ($249)
- **Name:** ZeroIntel Enterprise - Full Market Intelligence Report
- **URL slug:** zerointel-enterprise
- **Price:** $249
- **Description:** Full-scale AI market intelligence report analyzing your company vs up to 10 competitors. Includes positioning, pricing, features, SEO, market sizing, content strategy, and actionable recommendations. Priority delivery within 12 hours.
- **Custom fields on checkout:**
  - Your Company URL (required, text)
  - Competitor URLs (up to 10, comma-separated) (required, text)
  - Email for delivery (required, email)

## Setup Steps
1. Create Gumroad account at gumroad.com
2. Create each product with the details above
3. Enable custom fields for URL collection
4. Set up Gumroad webhook to notify when purchase completes
5. Update landing page CTA links with actual Gumroad product URLs
