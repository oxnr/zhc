## Usage Notes

- **Personalization is non-negotiable.** Research the company for 2 minutes before sending. Reference something specific — a recent launch, funding round, blog post, or competitor move.
- **One DM per person.** If they don't reply, don't follow up on DMs. Use email follow-up (Template 9) instead.
- **Best channels by persona:**
  - Indie hackers / solo founders: Twitter/X DMs (Templates 1-4)
  - Product managers / heads of product: LinkedIn (Template 6)
  - Series A-C founders: LinkedIn (Template 5) or cold email (Template 8)
- **Volume target:** 20-30 personalized DMs per day. Quality over quantity.
- **Track responses** in a spreadsheet: name, company, channel, template used, reply (y/n), converted (y/n).
