## FAQ Section

**How does ZeroIntel generate reports?**
We use AI to analyze publicly available data — websites, pricing pages, review sites, social presence, content strategy, SEO metrics, and more. The result is a structured competitive analysis that would take a human analyst days to compile.

**How is this different from hiring a consultant?**
Consultants charge $5K-$50K and take 2-6 weeks. We deliver in 24 hours for a fraction of the cost. For most startups and SMBs, our reports provide the strategic clarity you need without the enterprise price tag.

**What data sources do you use?**
Public websites, pricing pages, G2/Capterra reviews, social media profiles, job postings, blog/content output, SEO rankings, and web traffic estimates. We don't use any private or scraped data that violates terms of service.

**Can I get a sample report first?**
Yes. We publish a redacted sample report so you can see exactly what you're getting before you buy.

**How accurate are the reports?**
Our analysis is based on publicly available data as of the report date. We clearly label estimates vs. confirmed data points. Most customers report 85-90% alignment with their own internal competitive knowledge, plus new insights they hadn't considered.

**What if I'm not satisfied?**
If the report doesn't meet your expectations, email us within 48 hours and we'll either revise it or refund you. No games.

**Do you offer ongoing monitoring?**
Not yet — but it's coming. Right now each report is a point-in-time snapshot. Enterprise customers get a quarterly tracking framework to structure ongoing analysis.

**Can I analyze competitors outside my direct market?**
Absolutely. Some of our best insights come from adjacent-market analysis. Enter any company URLs you want compared.
